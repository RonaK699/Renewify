Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a creator on Renewify?

To become a creator, simply sign up for an account and navigate to your dashboard. From there, you can click on "Become a Creator" and follow the steps to set up your creator profile and start listing your products.

What types of products can I sell on Renewify?

Renewify is dedicated to upcycled and recycled products. You can sell any handmade items created from repurposed materials, including home decor, furniture, fashion accessories, art, and more. All products must be made primarily from recycled or upcycled materials.

How does shipping work for sellers?

As a seller, you're responsible for setting your shipping rates and policies. You can choose to offer free shipping, flat-rate shipping, or calculated shipping based on the buyer's location. We recommend using eco-friendly packaging materials to align with our mission.

What are the fees for selling on Renewify?

Renewify charges a 5% transaction fee on each sale. There are no listing fees or monthly subscription fees. We also offer a premium membership with reduced fees and additional features for $9.99 per month.

How can I track my environmental impact?

When you list a product, you can specify the approximate weight and type of materials that were diverted from waste. Your dashboard will automatically calculate and display your total environmental impact based on this information.
